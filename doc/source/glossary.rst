.. _glossary:

Glossary
========

.. glossary::
   :sorted:

   RISC-V
       RISC-V is a free and open ISA enabling a new era of processor innovation
       through open standard collaboration. Born in academia and research,
       RISC-V ISA delivers a new level of free, extensible software and hardware
       freedom on architecture, paving the way for the next 50 years of computing
       design and innovation.

   ISA
       An instruction set architecture (ISA) is an abstract model of a computer.
       It is also referred to as architecture or computer architecture.
